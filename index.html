Samy Vera

He judges. And hopes that the heavy weight placed on his frail shoulders may one day be lifted. False shell at present. The path to the bright future. He fails. The path from the cloudy past. He overrides. He runs away from their laws, and chooses to be alone, living life as he pleases, not bound by anyone... His true self lies beyond the freedom he attained. The self he has lost.
